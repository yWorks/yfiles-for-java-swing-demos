Step 18: Edge Hit Test

Take the thickness of an edge into account when checking if the edge was clicked.

Note: For the purpose of this demo, the thickness of edges is set to a high value.

Things to Try

  • Select an edge by clicking not in the middle but in the outer region. Even in a small range around the edge the hit test is positive. This is due to property HitTestRadius of class ICanvasContext. This space gets bigger if you zoom out to make the selection more convenient for the user.
  • Take a look at method isHit() in class MySimpleEdgeStyle.
