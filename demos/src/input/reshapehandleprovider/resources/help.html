Description

Customize the reshape behavior of nodes with custom configurations of the default IReshapeHandleProvider that are added to the lookup of the nodes.

Things to try

  • Select and resize the nodes.

Node Types

  • Red nodes cannot be resized. They don't display resize handles.
  • Green nodes show only four handles at the corners. During resizing, these handles maintain the aspect ratio of the node.
  • Gray nodes show only one handle at the bottom right corner. During resizing, this handle always maintains the center of the node.
  • Purple nodes show four handles at the corners and four different handles at the sides. During resizing, the corner handles always maintain the aspect ratio of the node while the border handles don't.
  • Orange nodes cannot extend beyond the black rectangle. They can only be resized so that the new bounding box stays withing the enclosing black rectangle.
  • Blue nodes combine the behavior of the orange and green nodes.
