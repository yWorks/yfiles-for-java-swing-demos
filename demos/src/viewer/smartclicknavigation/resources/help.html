Smart Click Navigation

This demo shows how to navigate in a large graph, especially when only a part of the graph is visible in the viewport. Clicking on items will move the graph so that the mouse pointer now points to a sensible focused item.

Things To Try

  • Click on an edge whose target node or source node is not currently visible in the viewport. Then you will notice that the focus will be moved to that previously-invisible node.
  • Click on an edge where both target and source node are visible. Then you will notice that the focus will be moved to the middle point of the edge.
  • Click on a node and you will notice that the focus will be moved to the center of the clicked node.
